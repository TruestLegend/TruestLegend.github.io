Enderdragon slain!

The enderdragon has been slain by three noble men called: Mechmanner, MatsDeJong09 en MythicalLyon. They went on this quest so they could obtain the fabled elytra and shulkerboxes. After a long and valiant fight by the trio they managed to end triumphant. There was a bit of a laugh when they realized that they went to a place with pillars and a flying lizard the size of a plane on september eleventh. And it was made even funnier when MythicalLyon used it's spyglass to inspect the dragon and it said: [Is it a plane?]. The fight itself did not take any lives, but a death has been located in the end, More about that later.
